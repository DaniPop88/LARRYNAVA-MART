Privacy Policy

At Larry Nava Family Mart, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information, why we collect it, and the steps we take to keep it safe.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as your name, address, phone number, and email when you place an order or sign up for updates.
  • Order history and transaction details to help us process and deliver your purchases efficiently.
  • Any feedback or inquiries you send us, which we use to improve our products and services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders, arrange delivery or pickup, and provide customer support.
  • To send you updates regarding your order status, special offers, or important store announcements (only with your consent).
  • To improve our store operations, website experience, and product selection based on your feedback and shopping trends.

How We Protect Your Data

We use secure systems and follow best practices to safeguard your personal information. Only authorized team members have access to customer data, and we never share your details with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and Web Analytics

Our website may use cookies to help you navigate and shop more easily. These cookies collect anonymous data to enhance your experience and support website analytics. You can adjust your browser settings to manage or block cookies at any time.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can request to review or update your personal information by contacting us directly.
  • If you wish to unsubscribe from our communications, simply let us know or click the unsubscribe link in our emails.
  • If you have concerns or questions about how your data is handled, please reach out for clarification.

Updates to Our Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the date of revision will be updated below.
